Jasper Media Player starter

This starter uses these packages when you build it:
- Microsoft.WindowsAppSDK
- LibVLCSharp
- VideoLAN.LibVLC.Windows

LibVLC/LibVLCSharp are VideoLAN projects. Keep their license notices when distributing your app.
Do not ship this app using the VLC name, cone logo, or branding unless you have the rights to do so.
